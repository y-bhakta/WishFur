Labrador

About Breed
Labradors are one of the most beloved dog breeds in the world, known for their intelligence, friendly nature, and loyalty. Originally bred as working dogs to retrieve fish and game, Labradors excel in various roles today, from family pets to service and therapy dogs.
Health Diet Tips
  • Feed Labradors high-quality kibble with balanced protein and fat.
  • Avoid overfeeding to prevent obesity.
  • Include occasional treats like carrots or blueberries.
Dailly Activities
  • Labradors need 1-2 hours of exercise daily.
  • Include activities like fetch, swimming, or brisk walks.
  • Mental stimulation games (e.g., puzzle toys) are essential.
Vet Visits & Vaccines
  • Schedule annual vet check-ups.
  • Keep up-to-date with vaccinations (rabies, distemper).
  • Watch for common issues like hip dysplasia or ear infections.
Behavioral Guidance
  • Start basic commands early: Sit, Stay, Come.
  • Use positive reinforcement (treats or praise).
  • Socialize with other dogs and environments to reduce anxiety.
Care & Hygiene
  • Brush their coat 2-3 times weekly to reduce shedding.
  • Bath every 6-8 weeks or as needed.
  • Regularly clean their ears and trim their nails.
